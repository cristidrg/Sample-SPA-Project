About Career Outcomes

The career outcomes snapshot provides insight into the employment and graduate education status of recent Northeastern baccalaureate graduates. Northeastern collects status information from students for approximately nine months after graduation through an annual Post-Graduation Destination Survey. The dashboard presents the results from this survey and is augmented by verifiable information from various university offices, LinkedIn, and the National Student Clearinghouse. The knowledge rates for graduating cohorts were: 75% for 2016 graduates and 76% for 2017 graduates. This website was created in April 2019.

Northeastern complies with National Association of Colleges and Employers (NACE) suggested career outcomes reporting practices and categories.

The content of this website is maintained by University Decision Support. Please e-mail surveys@northeastern.edu with questions or comments.
